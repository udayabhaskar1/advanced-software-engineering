What is K-Market?

K-Market is a grocery wholesaler and distributor who moves grocery and other supermarket products across various distribution channels.

How do I place an order online?

You can proceed to our to place an online order that will be delivered to your warehouse/ restaurant.

How much time does it take for the delivery to arrive at the restaurant?

It usually takes about 3-5 days for us to process your order.

What online payment solutions do you offer?

We offer online payment solutions such as PayPal, Credit/ Debit Card.

What are the quality of the products that you offer?

All our products are of high quality and adhere to the industry standard.

Can I physically pickup my order earlier than the promised delivery date?

Unfortunately it takes us 3-5 days to ensure the quality and availability of your order, so self-pickup is discouraged.
